About

Designing identities with clarity, distinction, and commercial value.

SD

Shantanu Dey is a freelance graphic designer specializing in logos, visual identity systems, advertising design, typography, and branding assets for ambitious businesses. Over the last 15+ years, he has developed distinctive marks for modern startups, established local brands, corporate businesses, food and hospitality concepts, lifestyle labels, and sports organizations.

His creative philosophy blends clean strategy with expressive form. Every identity is built to be memorable, adaptable, and visually premium across packaging, social media, signage, presentations, and campaigns.

As an independent creative partner, Shantanu works closely with clients who want thoughtful branding, professional presentation, and design that competes confidently on an international level.
